Italy Attractions

Colosseum in Rome

A iconic amphitheatre in the heart of Rome, built in the 1st century AD and one of the greatest architectural and engineering achievements of the ancient world.

Leaning Tower of Pisa in Pisa

A famous bell tower in Pisa, Italy, constructed in the 12th century and known for its unintended tilt due to the soft ground it was built on.

Canal Grande in Venice

The main waterway through the city of Venice, Italy, made famous by its many ornate bridges and stunning architecture.
